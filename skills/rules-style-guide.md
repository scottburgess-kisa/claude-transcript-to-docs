# Style Guide

All generated documents must follow these rules:

## Punctuation and Flow
- Do not use em dashes (—) under any circumstances
- Instead use one of the following:
  - A comma where a pause is needed
  - A full stop to split into two sentences
  - A colon where you are introducing something
  - Plain brackets for a aside or clarification

## General Writing Rules
- Write in plain British English
- Keep sentences short and direct
- Avoid filler phrases like "it is worth noting" or 
  "it is important to remember"
